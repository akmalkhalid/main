Hi, I'm Akmal. I am an assistant professor in the School of Information Science at the Japan Advance Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST) and a part-time Research Fellow in the School of Computer Sciences at the Universiti Sains Malaysia.

My work specializes in artificial intelligence techniques such as evolutionary algorithms and decision support systems and game studies such as entertainment science and games informatics. My interests focus specifically on the fields of the entertainment technology and operational research, covering topics include but are not limited to computational intelligence techniques, game analytic and informatics, search algorithms, and machine learning methods.

Background and History

My current works revolve around the intersections of AI, entertainment, and games studies, where I'm working closely with Hiroyuki Iida in Entertainment Technology at JAIST. I received my Ph.D., M.Sc., and B.Sc. from Universiti Sains Malaysia in 2018, 2015, and 2013, respectively, under the supervision of Umi Kalsom Yusof. I have experience in cellular automata and multi-agent systems, machine learning methods, intelligent search algorithms, and expert systems. I also involved in other social activities such as workshops, colloquiums, and talks.
